Flagship Projects & Experience

Doctoral Researcher – Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT)

The Entomoscope & DiversityScanner Ecosystem: Tackling the "taxonomic impediment" in biology, I lead the AI development for open-source, automated tools that identify, classify, and analyze insect species in real-time. My doctoral research is based at the Institute for Automation and Applied Informatics (IAI), Research Area Automated Image and Data Analysis (AIDA), Group Biomedical Engineering & Robotics (BER), KIT. My work powers the Entomoscope photomicroscope and DiversityScanner robotic sorter, enabling scalable, low-cost biodiversity monitoring. I also integrate advanced robotics (acoustic levitation, flexible grippers) for automated specimen handling, collaborating with leading scientific institutions.

  • Developed deep learning models for species identification, sex determination, and biomass estimation.
  • Embedded AI into hardware for real-time, user-friendly analysis.
  • Contributed to open-source, collaborative projects with global impact.

Data Scientist Consultant – Modis / Baker Hughes

Turbine Maintenance Optimization: In a high-stakes industrial setting, I designed predictive maintenance algorithms for gas turbines, analyzing sensor data to forecast component wear and optimize service schedules. This resulted in increased efficiency and profitability. I also led a cloud data migration project and developed process automation tools, training colleagues in digital workflows.

  • Built machine learning models for predictive analytics and optimization.
  • Managed secure cloud data migration and implemented data security protocols.
  • Empowered teams through training and citizen development tools.

Junior Data Scientist – Plasive Technologies

Carbon Stock Estimation from Satellite Imagery: Developed CNN-based semantic segmentation pipelines for remote sensing, enabling accurate, scalable carbon stock and biomass estimation from satellite data. Constructed multi-dimensional datasets and delivered actionable insights for environmental monitoring.

Machine Learning Intern – Kiwitron

Master's Thesis – Semi-Automatic Image Labeling: Created a novel system that uses 3D LiDAR point clouds to automate 2D image labeling, dramatically reducing manual annotation time and improving dataset quality for industrial AI applications.
